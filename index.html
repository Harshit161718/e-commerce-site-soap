About Us

Welcome to Soapanic, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2021 by Viral Vegad and Harshit Vasoya, Soapanic has come a long way from its beginnings in a Surat. We now serve customers all over the Gujarat, the world and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the soap industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
